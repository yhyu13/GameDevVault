                    I see, the reason is because 4 transforms your original function from the space domain to the frequency domain. 6 is the inverse transformation. The only reason to use Spherical harmonics is because convolution can be calculated in frequencies more efficiently, that is important because we need real time framerates in games. So what we do with spherical harmonics is:
                    1) transform to frequencies,
                    2) apply convolution (cheaper),
                    3) transform back to space (or time). It looks like more work, but it has been greatly optimized.
